002 : ParaSpeak: A Speaking System for Paralyzed

IoT Based System that will help the Paralyzed patient communicate through blowing air into the system.

"Some People Are Walking Around With Full Use of Their Bodies And They're More Paralyzed Than I Am"

Christopher Reeve

People with disabilities face a lot of difficulties in their life & not being able to convey their thoughts is the most difficult thing for them. This was our core idea of focus on how to easen their pain and built a system which is really efficient in all domains. Hence, we built a simple micro-controller based air blowing system that can display different messages for the patient on LCD Screen or on smartphones.
The work on this system is still in progress.
