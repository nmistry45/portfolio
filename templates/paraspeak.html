007 : ParaSpeak: A Speaking System for Paralyzed

IoT Based System that will help the Paralyzed patient communicate by blowing air into the system.

"Some People Are Walking Around With Full Use of Their Bodies And They're More Paralyzed Than I Am"

Christopher Reeve
People with disabilities face a lot of difficulties in their life & not being able to convey their thoughts is the most difficult thing for them. This was our core idea of focusing on how to ease their pain and build a system that is efficient in all domains. Hence, we built a simple microcontroller-based air-blowing system that can display different messages for the patient on LCD screens or smartphones.
