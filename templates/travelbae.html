003 : TravelBae

Suggesting better choices for travel and providing the essential information.

“To Travel is to Live!”

Hans Christian Anderson
Many websites provide either itineraries, transportation, accommodation, etc. But what if you get all this information for your travel at just one location? TravelBae is one such stop where you will find all information regarding your favorite destinations including Mumbai, Toronto, Paris, and many more. Created using the MERN stack, you can check out the itineraries for that place, create your things to carry, look at suggested hotels and flights and also write a blog or read blogs written by other people. Additionally, you can create your travel plan by adding various things to a single plan and view it comfortably through your profile dashboard.


GitHub Link:- https://github.com/nmistry45/travelbae
Website Link:- https://travel-bae.herokuapp.com/
