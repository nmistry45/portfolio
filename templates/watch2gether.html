003 : Watch2Gether

Helping people make good choices for watching their favorite content together.

“Some people can’t believe in themselves until someone else believes in them first.”

Sean Maguire, Good Will Hunting
Sometimes we spend countless hours correctly finding the movie or TV show we want to watch while enjoying a bowl of hot popcorn. Hence, we developed a web application using the MERN stack that would help individuals make informed decisions to watch their favorite content. The website boosts some informative charts that provide an overall view of the ratings of the movie or ratings of each season for a TV series. You can also go to particular content and join a watch group where you can post your crazy theories and even like someone's post and comment on it.
